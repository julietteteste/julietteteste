juliette teste

about

art direction & consulting.

After graduating from Ecole Duperré & Institut Français de la Mode, Juliette began her career at YSL image team, then Dior Homme.

She started her own company, Signe, specialized in fashion & beauty brand imagery in 2016.

Still-life images, print & digital brand content, set & volume installations made with dedication, exigency and a keen eye on the art world, are at the core of Signe's propositions.

Client list includes Chanel Parfums Beauté, IFM, La Compagnie de Provence, Le Monde d’Hermès, M le Monde, YSL.

Currently based in Paris.
